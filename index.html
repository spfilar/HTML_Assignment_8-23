Welcome to the Rat Kingdom

This site is dedicated to the dissemation of information about rats who have been important throughout history. Below, you will find information about some of the most famous rats in recent history. On the left, you will find links about how to support rats and the rat community.

Rizzo the Rat is a character from the Muppets who is a wiseguy known for making fun of other characters and utilizing comic relief. He is portrayed to have many different sides in that he sometimes takes advantage of others, but also have a sensitive side. He is well-known for narrating The Muppet Christmas Carol.

Master Splinter is a (mostly) fictional character who is portrayed in the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles comics. He ends up going to New York City to live after his master is killed and is transformed by ooze along with four baby turtles he finds in the sewers. He raises them as his sons and trains them in ninjutsu. Grade A badass.

Templeton is a character from the book Charlotte's Web by author E.B. White. He is a greedy rat who helps Charlotte devise messages to write in her web to help Wilbur. He is like the quintessential mooch, and only helps out when it is beneficial to him.
